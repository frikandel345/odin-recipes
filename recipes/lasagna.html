Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an italian recipe based on pasta sheets and marinara sauce.

The different ingredients are often one-by-one layered on top of each other to create a nice evenly layered dish.

There are many types of lasagna. The recipe below is a tomato-sauce based lasagna

Ingredients

  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagne sheets
  • Vegetables, f.e.: Mushrooms, Zucchini, Bell peppers etc.
  • (Vegan) Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables.
  2. Saute the vegetables./li>
  3. Layer the sauc, vegetables and lasagna (in this order).
  4. End the last layer with sauce and sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Put the dish in the oven for a certain temperature and time until fully cooked.
  6. Remove the dish from the over and let it cool down slightly before serving, enjoy!
